What $CLRV Does

// every holder action that requires or rewards the token

Oracle Challenge Stake
When a market resolves incorrectly, users stake CLRV to open a formal Adversarial Challenge. Valid challengers earn fees from the slashed oracle's stake. Invalid challenges lose their stake to the expert panel.
Adversarial Oracle
DAO Governance
CLRV holders vote on protocol upgrades, fee parameters, market category additions, and treasury deployment. Staked CLRV weight is amplified by on-chain Calibration Score — accurate forecasters govern more.
DAO · 3%
Liquidity Provider Rewards
LPs staking CLRV into complex multi-leg markets earn scaled CLRV rewards. A 10-leg market LP earns 10× the base incentive rate. This bootstraps depth where it is most needed — the novel combo markets.
Staking · 10%
Calibration Score Rewards
Resolvers who achieve top-quartile Calibration Scores in their domain earn CLRV fees for participating in dispute panels. Accuracy is financially incentivised from day one — skin in the game at every layer.
Incentives · 15%
Prediction Insurance Pools
CLRV deposited into Insurance Pools backs coverage against oracle error and resolution ambiguity. Pool depositors earn insurance premiums as passive yield — proportional to their share of the coverage backing.
Treasury · 15%
Airdrop & Bootstrap Rewards
Early adopters, testnet participants, and Shadow Portfolio power users receive CLRV airdrops. This seeds initial DAO participation without requiring capital — reputation and engagement are the entry criteria.
Airdrops · 5%

A token with a
reason to exist.

$CLRV is not a speculative asset issued ahead of a product. Every allocation maps to a function that the Clairvoyance protocol requires to operate: staking to make oracle challenges credible, governance to let calibrated forecasters set protocol direction, insurance pools to make resolution guarantees meaningful, and liquidity incentives to make complex markets actually tradeable.

The community owns 30%. Investors hold 25% — subject to cliff and vesting schedules that align their interests with long-term protocol health, not short-term listing prices. The treasury holds 15% under DAO control to fund development the platform cannot yet anticipate.

The Clairvoyance thesis is that accurate prediction has economic value — and that the infrastructure for expressing, recording, and settling that prediction should be transparent, composable, and resistant to manipulation. $CLRV is the mechanism that makes that resistance real.
